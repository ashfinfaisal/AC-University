Principal's Message

Prof. Dr. Faisal Muneer K,
Welcome to AC University, an Institution that rests on a strong academic foundation of 9 years, blended with a modern approach that seeks to mould young men and women into successful world leaders.I have recently joined the Wilson family and feel privileged to lead such a vibrant College that offers extensive opportunities.

We are proud of our caring, inclusive ethos where we set high standards and expectations of our students to achieve their very best. Under my leadership, and as a team working together, we strongly promote academic achievement among our students. Our academic standards continue to improve year on year and the teaching methodology helps to engage and inspire, providing pupils with the necessary skills to flourish in an ever-changing world..
